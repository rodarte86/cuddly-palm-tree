Behind Alpha Uno

What makes us unique

Rodney Martin Jr.

Owner/Founder

Veronica Rodarte

CEO/Manager

Both Veronica and I (Rodney) are a couple who are very passionate about fitness, having healthy lifestyle, and most importantly helping others! We loved it so much, that we started helping others achieve their goals (for weightlifting, toning, losing weight, and simply being more mobile) from our own garage. We converted our garage into a home gym and opened our doors in 2018 to members who wanted to change or simply wanted to use this lifestyle as a hobby. We love seeing people accomplish their goals and watching the expressions they make after achieving something they thought were never possible!

We both come from different backgrounds of fitness. I Rodney, am all-around athlete and trainer. I have always been into sports and fitness since the age of 8. I knew my career one day would evolve around that. I now have the experience of having competed, trained in several sports such as track and field, marathons, triathlons, martial arts, basketball, football, soccer, CF, and Olympic lifting just to name a few. I am also a certified trainer.

Veronica, (our Wellness Coach and mom of 1) works in the medical field behind a computer 8 hours a day. With her busy schedule she still manages to fit her passion for helping others. She started her fitness journey in 2014. She struggled with losing weight as she always seemed to plateau, but noticed that she had to make some changes in her workout routine and in the kitchen. Since then she lost weight, gained muscle and her passion for helping others grew. She has assisted several of our members achieve their goals outside the gym and implementing healthy eating habits in their kitchens.

We want to continue making a change in our community. So if you or anybody you know are interested come and try us out! We work with all ages and background in fitness, whether you are novice, intermediate, advance or a master. Wherever you start we will make sure you move on to the next level.

We promise that our environment will be nothing but friendly. We will help YOU accomplish YOUR GOALS!
